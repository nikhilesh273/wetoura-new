Destinations

Andaman Islands

Explore pristine beaches, historic Cellular Jail, and crystal-clear waters. Experience Havelock Island, Radhanagar Beach, and the Light & Sound show in this 4-day tropical paradise.

Kerala - God's Own Country

Journey through Kochi's heritage, Munnar's tea gardens, Thekkady's wildlife, and Alappuzha's backwaters. Experience Kolukkumalai sunrise, houseboat cruise, and spice plantations in this 6-day adventure.

Lakshadweep Island Escape

Discover the pristine beauty of Lakshadweep — a paradise of turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant coral reefs. Enjoy snorkeling, scuba diving, and serene island life in this tropical getaway.

Magical Malaysia Discovery

Explore the vibrant cities, lush rainforests, and tropical islands of Malaysia. From the iconic Petronas Towers in Kuala Lumpur to the beaches of Langkawi and the culture of Penang, it’s a journey of colors and contrasts.

Thailand Tropical Escape

Experience the vibrant culture and stunning landscapes of Thailand. From the bustling streets of Bangkok to the serene beaches of Phuket and the lush jungles of Chiang Mai, enjoy a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation.

Heavenly Kashmir Retreat

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir — snow-capped mountains, serene Dal Lake, and lush valleys. Enjoy shikara rides, houseboat stays, and explore the enchanting meadows of Gulmarg and Pahalgam.

Kazakhstan Explorer

Journey through Kazakhstan’s vast landscapes, from the modern cityscape of Almaty to the dramatic steppes and the Altai Mountains. Discover nomadic culture, pristine lakes, and unique natural wonders on this unforgettable adventure.

Mystical Meghalaya Adventure

Explore the breathtaking landscapes of Meghalaya, the “Abode of Clouds.” Wander through lush hills, cascading waterfalls, living root bridges, and charming villages while experiencing the rich culture of the Khasi, Garo, and Jaintia tribes.

Bhutan Cultural Retreat

Discover the serene beauty of Bhutan, the Land of the Thunder Dragon. Explore majestic monasteries, lush valleys, and the vibrant culture of the Himalayan kingdom while immersing yourself in peaceful mountain landscapes.

Bali Island Paradise

Escape to Bali’s tropical paradise with its pristine beaches, lush rice terraces, and vibrant culture. Experience temple visits, surfing, and the island’s serene landscapes for an unforgettable getaway.

Vietnam Scenic Journey

Discover the charm of Vietnam with its emerald rice fields, limestone cliffs, and ancient cities. Cruise through Ha Long Bay, wander the lantern-lit streets of Hoi An, and savor authentic Vietnamese cuisine on this unforgettable adventure.

Manali Mountain Escape

Embrace the charm of Manali, nestled in the heart of the Himalayas. Enjoy snow-covered peaks, scenic valleys, and thrilling adventures like paragliding, river rafting, and trekking for a perfect mountain getaway.

Phuket & Krabi Islands

Explore Thailand's most beautiful beach destinations with crystal-clear waters, limestone cliffs, and tropical paradise. Experience Phi Phi Islands, James Bond Island, and pristine beaches in this 5-day adventure.
